WHO I AM

I'm Bryan, a full-stack engineer, designer, and founder of InfiniChat and Shuttrr. I love crafting digital products that blend creativity, performance, and purpose. My curiosity drives me to explore every stage of development, from concept to launch, bringing ideas to life through TypeScript, React Native, Next.js, and serverless architectures like Convex. I earned my B.S. in Computer Science from the University of Florida and have built projects like InfiniChat, Shuttrr, and Anywhere—all focused on creating seamless, meaningful experiences across web and mobile platforms.
