I'm a full-stack software engineer who thrives at the intersection of design and code. My passion lies in building products that are both technically sophisticated and beautifully crafted—tools that solve real problems with zero friction.

I graduated from the University of Florida with a BS in Computer Science, where I developed a foundation in systems thinking and algorithmic problem-solving. Over the past 4+ years, I've specialized in modern web technologies—TypeScript, Next.js, React, Supabase—and have become particularly excited about AI integration and creating seamless user experiences.
